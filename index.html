Hi, I’m Ayşenur Onaran—a Webflow Developer and Visual Designer with a growing focus on front-end development. With three years of experience in design, I’ve built intuitive interfaces, marketing assets, and seamless user experiences. I specialize in Webflow development, creating dynamic, high-performing websites while integrating custom code when needed.

At ElevateHT, I developed a six-step ROI calculator from scratch using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, solving complex problems that standard tools couldn’t handle. This project deepened my interest in coding, leading me to expand my skills beyond Webflow.

Now, I’m refining my expertise in JavaScript, Tailwind, and React, with a strong interest in micro-animations using GSAP to enhance interactivity and user experience. This website—version 1.0—marks my transition into full front-end development, and I’ll continue evolving with each new version.

Thanks for stopping by—let’s build something great together!
